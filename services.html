The Scope For Value Addition

Areas of work

Capacity building development

  • Enabling local artisans to turn their skill into a sustainable vocation.
  • Ensuring cluster development among craft and handloom weaving communities, within the reach of prime beneficiaries.
  • Conducting design thinking workshops for various Indian craft communities.

Marketplace development

  • E-commerce website development for rural enterprises.
  • Promoting their interaction and skills in public space.
  • Increase civic participation through various exhibitions, annual or seasonal events.
  • Engaging local youth in the community and enhance their technical skills & knowledge.
  • Giving visibility to traditional artists, craftsmen, cultures through a platform.

Documentation

  • Documenting strategic and creative processes of development sector projects which include process documentation, subject selection, well defining of key indicators, knowledge of research, writing skills & ethical approach and seeing them to completion with desired results.
  • Video/photo documentation of development projects, beneficiary experiences, project impact assessment and success stories, crafts and case studies.

Product development for social needs

  • Working within our team and with various social ventures, communities, rural entrepreneurs and social innovators to create design experiences.
  • Providing research-led, sustainable and human-centric design approach to build products and user interactive spaces.
  • Branding identities.
  • Implementing projects in the field of arts, crafts, cultures, spaces and streets, innovation and rural entrepreneurship.

Implementation

  • Overseeing implementation and monitoring of CSR projects, NGOs initiatives, Government projects, startup ideas and design led innovation projects.
  • Implementing projects in the field of arts, crafts, cultures, spaces and streets, innovation and rural entrepreneurship.
  • Working closely with the leadership and program teams of various stakeholders, funders, researchers and academicians.
  • Leading the development, adaptation, testing and mainstreaming of best/good practices which are required within design led social projects.

Awareness campaigns

  • Designing social media campaigns to create awareness regarding social and cultural causes.
  • Conducting workshops and meets to address socially relevant issues in collaboration.
